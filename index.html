About Enkaji Travels

At Enkaji Travels, we believe every journey should feel like home. Inspired by the Maasai word Enkaji, meaning "home," we offer personalized and unforgettable travel experiences across East Africa and beyond. Whether you are exploring breathtaking safaris, cultural gems, or vibrant cities, our dedicated team ensures comfort, safety, and authentic adventure every step of the way.

With expertly crafted itineraries, well-maintained tour vans, and a passion for showcasing the beauty of Africa, Enkaji Travels is your trusted partner for memorable journeys.

Custom Tour Planning & Booking
We design tailor-made tours to fit your dreams and budget. From wildlife safaris, cultural experiences, beach holidays, to city explorations we handle all your bookings for accommodation, activities, flights, and transportation.
Tour Van Rentals with Professional Drivers
Travel in comfort and style with our modern, fully-equipped tour vans. Whether you need airport transfers, day trips, or multi-day excursions, our experienced drivers and guides ensure a safe and enjoyable journey.
Group, Corporate & Special Event Travel
We organize travel for corporate retreats, school trips, group tours, and special events. Our team manages all the logistics so you can focus on creating lasting memories with your team, family, or friends.
