About Diani Beach

Diani Beach is Kenya's most beautiful and popular beach destination, stretching for 17 kilometers along the Indian Ocean coast. Located 30 kilometers south of Mombasa, this pristine white sand beach is consistently ranked among the best beaches in Africa and the world.

Did you know?

Diani Beach has been voted Africa’s leading beach destination for several years in a row, and its coral reefs are home to dolphins, sea turtles, and vibrant marine life.

The beach is characterized by its powdery white sand, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and swaying palm trees that create a perfect tropical paradise. The warm Indian Ocean waters are ideal for swimming, snorkeling, diving, and a variety of water sports throughout the year.

Diani Beach offers the perfect blend of relaxation and adventure, with world-class resorts, boutique hotels, and beachfront villas catering to every type of traveler. The area is also rich in culture, with nearby attractions including Shimba Hills National Reserve and the historic town of Mombasa.

Quick Facts

  • Location: Kwale County, Kenya
  • Length: 17 km of pristine beach
  • Best Time: June-October, January-March
  • Climate: Tropical, 25-32°C year-round
  • Access: Flight to Mombasa (1 hour)

Beach Activities & Attractions

Water Sports

Diani Beach offers excellent conditions for water sports including kiteboarding, windsurfing, jet skiing, and parasailing. The coral reefs just offshore provide world-class snorkeling and diving opportunities with vibrant marine life.

Beach Relaxation

Unwind on the pristine white sands with beach massages, yoga sessions, or simply relax under the shade of palm trees. The gentle ocean breeze and sound of waves create the perfect environment for ultimate relaxation.

Cultural Experiences

Visit nearby Shimba Hills National Reserve for wildlife viewing, explore the historic Fort Jesus in Mombasa, or take a traditional dhow cruise to experience the rich Swahili culture and history of the region.

Best Time to Visit

June - October

Dry Season: Perfect weather with clear skies, warm temperatures, and excellent conditions for water sports and beach activities.

January - March

Short Dry Season: Warm and pleasant weather with fewer tourists. Great for whale watching and marine activities.

Year-Round

Always Beautiful: Diani Beach enjoys pleasant weather throughout the year. Even during the rainy seasons, there are plenty of sunny days.
