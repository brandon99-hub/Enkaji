About Tsavo

Tsavo National Park is Kenya’s largest protected area, split into Tsavo East and Tsavo West. Covering over 22,000 square kilometers, it is a land of dramatic contrasts—red-dusted elephants, volcanic hills, lava flows, lush riverine forests, and endless savannah.

Did you know?

Tsavo’s elephants are famous for their red color, which comes from dust-bathing in the park’s iron-rich soils. Tsavo is also home to the world’s longest lava flow—the Yatta Plateau!

The park is famous for its huge herds of elephants, the legendary man-eaters of Tsavo, and its wild, untamed landscapes. It’s a paradise for adventurers, photographers, and anyone seeking a true wilderness experience.

Tsavo is less crowded than other parks, offering a sense of solitude and discovery. The Galana River, Mzima Springs, and the Yatta Plateau (the world’s longest lava flow) are just a few of its natural wonders.

Quick Facts

  • Location: Southeastern Kenya
  • Size: 22,000+ sq km
  • Best Time: June-October, January-February
  • Wildlife: Elephants, lions, leopards, rhinos, hippos, crocodiles
  • Access: Road or train from Nairobi/Mombasa

Wildlife & Top Experiences

Red Elephants of Tsavo

Tsavo is famous for its herds of red-dusted elephants, who bathe in the park’s iron-rich soils. You’ll also find lions, leopards, cheetahs, giraffes, zebras, and over 500 bird species.

Mzima Springs & Galana River

Visit the crystal-clear Mzima Springs, home to hippos and crocodiles, or explore the Galana River’s lush banks for wildlife and birdwatching.

Safari Adventures

Enjoy game drives, guided walks, and even night safaris. Tsavo’s vastness means you can explore for days and rarely see another vehicle.

Best Time to Visit

June - October

Dry Season: Best wildlife viewing as animals gather at water sources. Pleasant weather and clear skies.

January - February

Short Dry Season: Good wildlife viewing, fewer tourists, and beautiful landscapes.

Year-Round

Always Wild: Tsavo offers adventure and wildlife throughout the year, with each season offering something unique.
